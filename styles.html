Prominent Styles

There are a wide variety of styles of dance which date back hundreds of years among many different cultures. These are incredibly important to their history and tradition. In America, there are several styles that are the most well known and studied.

Ballet

A very formal and precise, yet elegant and graceful, arrangement of steps and movements performed typically to classical music.

Jazz

A theatrical, upbeat style that is performed to popular or jazz music.

Tap

A style utilizing metal taps affixed to the heel and sole of the dancer's shoes, to perform clear and syncopated sounds.

Hip Hop

A street dancing style that uses freestyle and rythmic movements to heavier beats.

Modern

A theatrical and expressional style which challenges the traditional values and structure of ballet.

Contemporary

A combination of jazz and ballet styles which is more interpretive and fluid, often conveying stories or emotion.

Ballroom

A dance performed by couples, typically male and female, which combines sets of steps in a closed, held position.
